EUROPUBHEALTH+ FULLY FUNDED MASTER'S SCHOLARSHIP

A Master's program in Public Health in 2–3 European countries with a full Erasmus Mundus scholarship

APPLY NOW


The BIODAS Team is pleased to introduce the Erasmus Mundus Europubhealth+ Scholarship – a fully-funded master's program in Public Health for international students, allowing them to study in 2–3 European countries, receive a dual degree from 2 leading universities, and receive financial support of €1,400/month. Notably, the program accepts students from other fields – whether you have a background in Medicine, Pharmacy, Biotechnology, or Economics, Law, or Sociology, you can apply.

Official website: https://www.europubhealth.org

1. Introduction to the Europubhealth+ program

Europubhealth+ is a 2-year (120 ECTS) Erasmus Mundus Master's program in Public Health, recognized by the European Commission as a "Master of Excellence" since 2006. The program is taught by a consortium of 8 leading European universities, offering students the opportunity to study in multiple countries and receive a globally recognized dual degree.

The program welcomes international students with a Bachelor's degree (minimum 180 ECTS) or equivalent, from all academic fields:

  • Health sciences: Medicine, Pharmacy, Biology, Nursing, Biomedical Engineering...
  • Social sciences: Sociology, Economics, Law, Political Science, Anthropology...
  • Business administration and related fields.

Objective: To train multidisciplinary professionals who can analyze and address public health challenges on a global scale, and propose and implement evidence-based policies to improve public health.

2. Why choose Europubhealth+

  • ✅ Full scholarship: €1,400/month + tuition fee waiver + health insurance
  • ✅ Dual degree from 2 EU universities, internationally recognized
  • ✅ Study in 2–3 European countries, a unique multicultural experience
  • ✅ Accepts students from other fields – no medical background required
  • ✅ Includes biostatistics, epidemiology, health policy, health economics
  • ✅ Global alumni network in public health

3. Study pathway

Year 1: Foundational knowledge of Public Health (60 ECTS)

Students choose to study at 1 of the following 4 universities:

  • University of Sheffield – UK (English)
  • University College Dublin – Ireland (English)
  • University of Granada – Spain (Spanish)
  • University of Liège – Belgium (French)

Year 2: Specialization and Practice (60 ECTS)

  • Maastricht University – Netherlands (English)
  • Jagiellonian University – Poland (English)
  • EHESP School of Public Health – France (Rennes)
  • University of Bordeaux ISPED – France (Bordeaux)

The content includes specialized modules, an internship (2–6 months), and a master's thesis.

4. Admission requirements

  • Bachelor's degree ≥180 ECTS in any field.
  • No medical background required.
  • IELTS ≥6.5 or equivalent (depending on the pathway).
  • Application documents: degree & transcript, CV, motivation letter, 2 letters of recommendation, language certificate.

6. Erasmus Mundus Scholarship value

  • Living allowance: €1,400/month (24 months = €33,600)
  • 100% tuition fee waiver
  • Full-time health insurance
  • Total value: approximately €50,000–55,000/2 years

7. Application guide

  1. Create an account at emundus.izp.cm-uj.krakow.pl
  2. Complete the online application (9 sections: education, experience, motivation, documents...)
  3. Upload required and optional documents.
  4. Online interview (02/18/2026, 15–20 minutes).

8. Selection criteria

  • Motivation and career goals – 30%
  • Academic achievement – 25%
  • Professional/volunteer experience – 20%
  • International experience – 15%
  • Communication skills – 10%

9. Reasons to choose Europubhealth+

  • Study at 8 leading EU universities – recognized as a "Master of Excellence".
  • International dual degree, study in 2–3 countries.
  • Integrates biostatistics, epidemiology, data science.
  • Global alumni network, internships at WHO, NGOs, research institutes.
  • Strong financial support and wide career opportunities.
