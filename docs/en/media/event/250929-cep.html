<h3>1. Program Introduction</h3>
<p>
  Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine, in collaboration with Hainan International Medical Center, cordially invites universities and academic institutions worldwide to nominate outstanding students and graduates to participate in the 
  <strong>China Experience Program (CEP)</strong>, funded by the China International Talent Exchange Foundation.
</p>

<h3>2. Program Objectives</h3>
<p>
  The CEP is a fully funded short-term exchange program that aims to bring together top young talents from around the world to experience China's diverse culture and dynamic innovation environment. 
  The program's goal is to build a community of future global leaders with a deep understanding of China and the values of international cooperation.
</p>

<h3>3. Program Highlights (1 week)</h3>
<!-- CALL OUT: Highlights -->
<div class="event-callout">
  <ul>
    <li>Full scholarship: Includes international airfare, accommodation, meals, and all program-related expenses in China.</li>
    <li>In-depth academic program: Themed seminars, basic Chinese language and culture classes, along with practical activities and interactive workshops.</li>
    <li>Curated cultural experience: Visits to iconic historical and cultural landmarks, providing a deep experience of Chinese heritage.</li>
    <li>High-value networking: Opportunity to interact directly with faculty and students from top universities, along with a practical visit to an innovative Chinese enterprise or research institute.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<h3>4. Nomination Criteria</h3>
<!-- CALL OUT: Nomination Criteria -->
<div class="event-callout">
  <ul>
    <li>Age: Born after January 1, 1990</li>
    <li>Education: Graduated with a bachelor's, master's, or doctoral degree within the last 3 years.</li>
    <li>Personal qualities: Excellent academic record, leadership potential, and a keen interest in cultural and scientific exchange between China and the world.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<h3>5. Nomination Process</h3>
<p>Schools or organizations can submit a list of candidates via email, including:</p>
<!-- CALL OUT: Nomination Process -->
<div class="event-callout">
  <ul>
    <li>Full name (as on passport)</li>
    <li>Date of birth</li>
    <li>Passport number</li>
    <li>Highest degree obtained (e.g., Bachelor of Public Health, 2023) and date of issue</li>
  </ul>
