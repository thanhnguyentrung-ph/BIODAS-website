  <h2>1. Program Introduction</h2>
  <p>The program is <strong>recruiting for one fully funded PhD position</strong> in the field of <strong>basic research on infectious diseases</strong>. The position is fully funded for 4 years by DAAD, within the framework of the PACE-UP project. The candidate will pursue a PhD at the <strong>Bernhard Nocht Institute for Tropical Medicine (BNITM)</strong>, in collaboration with the <strong>University of Hamburg</strong>, Germany.</p>

  <h2>2. Research Topic</h2>
  <p><em>Development of an ex vivo mosquito midgut model to simulate the infection process of insect-borne viruses.</em>  
  The study will examine the effects of temperature, infection dose, and virus viability in gut tissue. The results will be evaluated for their applicability to different virus groups and the potential for expansion to research on virus inhibitors.</p>
