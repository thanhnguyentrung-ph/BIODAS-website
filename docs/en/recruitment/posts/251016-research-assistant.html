The Biomedical Research and Data Science Institute (BIODAS) is recruiting for the position of Research Assistant (Full-time) for candidates with strong writing skills, solid academic thinking, and a passion for compiling and developing high-quality research content.

This position focuses on domestic and international writing and publishing activities, including:

  • Writing and editing scientific paper manuscripts (manuscripts, review papers, book chapters‚Ä¶).
  • Drafting research proposals, proposals and research grants.
  • Editing, translating and publishing scientific documents, reports, books and specialized publications.

Suitable for candidates with a passion for research, coherent writing skills, good critical thinking, carefulness and a desire to develop a long-term career in the field of academic writing and international publication.

Specific tasks

  • Participate in writing and editing research proposals, proposals, and grant applications.
  • Draft international journal articles, scientific reports, monographs, training materials and academic communications.
  • Translate and academically edit specialized documents (English - Vietnamese and vice versa).
  • Synthesize, analyze, cite and manage reference materials (EndNote, Zotero‚Ä¶).
  • Coordinate with research teams and experts to standardize content and unify scientific writing style.
  • Participate in supporting the preparation of materials for training courses, workshops, and internal publications.
  • Perform other academic tasks according to the Institute's research orientation.

Candidate requirements

  • Graduates in Medicine, Pharmacy, Health Sciences, Biomedical Sciences, Medical Sociology or related fields.
  • Proficient in academic writing skills in English and Vietnamese.
  • Experience in compiling or publishing scientific articles domestically or internationally.
  • Skills in writing research proposals, proposals or scientific reports.
  • Proficient in searching, synthesizing, and managing documents (EndNote, Zotero‚Ä¶).
  • Priority is given to candidates with a basic understanding of research methods, statistics or data analysis (Excel, SPSS, Stata, R).

Benefits

  • Income: Fixed salary by agreement + bonus based on published products + completion progress bonus.
  • Direct guidance by internationally experienced researchers and academic writing experts.
  • Opportunity for in-depth training in academic writing, article writing, proposals and international funding applications.
  • Opportunity to co-author scientific articles and publications of BIODAS.
  • Support for using BIODAS data for theses, personal research or independent articles.
  • Professional, flexible, international publication-oriented and long-term development working environment.
