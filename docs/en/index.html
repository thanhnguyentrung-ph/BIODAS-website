BIODAS — Institute of Biomedical Data and Science Research

Welcome to BIODAS
Institute of Biomedical Research and Data Science

BIODAS is a specialized research unit in the field of biomedicine and data science in Vietnam.

We are on a mission to connect hospitals, research institutes, public and private healthcare units at home and abroad, to promote medical research, technology development, and improve the quality of medical treatment for the community.

BIODAS’s Mission, Vision, and Core Values

Mission
BIODAS connects Vietnamese intelligence with global science, provides comprehensive data solutions, accompanies domestic and foreign medical units, promotes biomedical research and innovation for a prosperous and humane future.
Vision
BIODAS pioneers scientific research & biomedical data, accompanies the nation's era of growth, for the health and quality of life of the community.
Core Values
BIODAS upholds Transparent Science, Technological Innovation, Collaborative Connection, Research Ethics, and For the Community.
